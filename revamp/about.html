Our Story

District 2-E2 Eyeglass Recycling Center was founded in 2013 with a simple vision: to ensure that no one should go without proper vision care due to cost.

Who We Are

What started as a small group of passionate volunteers has grown into a thriving 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that serves mission partners across 20+ countries. Every single member of our team is a volunteer, completely dedicated to making a global impact.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We operate with complete transparency.
  • Compassion: We care deeply about improving lives globally.
  • Sustainability: We reduce electronic and glass waste.
  • Community: Everything runs on the power of volunteers.

Leadership Board

Our dedicated board ensures we stay highly operational.

Our Core Partners

We are extremely grateful for the support of our service partners across the state.
