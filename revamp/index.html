Giving Sight
to the world.

We collect, process, and distribute recycled eyeglasses to those in need across 40+ countries. Join us in bringing vision and hope globally.

Volunteer Today Find Drop-Offs
2.2 Billion

People globally have a near or distance vision impairment. Of these, at least 1 billion cases have not yet been addressed.

It costs less than 10¢ US to provide a pair of recycled eyeglasses. This simple gift changes someone's life—providing a child better education, or an adult critical employment opportunities.

Our Mission

"To provide eyeglasses without cost to those missions who have a method of determining the refractive status of the eye."

Impact by the Numbers

Our recycled glasses support Humanitarian and Medical Missions worldwide.
