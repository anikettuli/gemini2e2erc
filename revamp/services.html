The Protocol

From donation drop-off to final distribution across the globe, our meticulous 4-step recycling protocol ensures every usable pair of eyeglasses finds a second life.

01.

Sorting

Eyeglasses are carefully sorted into categories: non-prescription style, nearsighted (minus diopter), farsighted (plus diopter), bifocal, and advanced progressive lenses.

02.

Washing & Drying

Glasses are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized in specialized baths to ensure they're medically safe, hygienic, and perfectly ready for a new owner.

03.

Measuring Prescription

Using sophisticated lensometers, we determine the exact optical prescription of each clean lens down to the quarter diopter accuracy.

04.

Quality Control & Storage

Processed glasses are sorted one last time into our highly organized storage library, labeled and ready for immediate mission distribution in the field.

Accepted Items

We accept a wide variety of optical and sensory-related donations to support our extensive medical mission footprint.

What we recycle:

  • All prescription eyeglasses
  • Over-the-counter reading glasses
  • Prescription & non-prescription sunglasses
  • Broken or damaged glasses (for scrap parts)
  • Functioning hearing aids
