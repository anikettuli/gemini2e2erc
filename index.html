Our 4-Step Recycling Process

We have a dedicated process to ensure used eyeglasses are ready for a new owner.

Step 1: Sorting

Eyeglasses are sorted into categories, such as non-prescription, nearsightedness (minus), farsightedness (plus), bifocal, trifocal, progressive, and prism.

Step 2: Washing & Drying

Glasses are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized to ensure they're ready for their new owner.

Step 3: Measuring the Prescription

We use specialized equipment to determine the exact prescription of each lens.

Step 4: Final Sorting & Storage

The processed glasses are sorted one last time and stored, ready for distribution.

About Lions District 2-E2 ERC

A community-driven organization dedicated to recycling eyeglasses and serving global mission partners.

Our Story

District 2-E2 Eyeglass Recycling Center was founded in 2013 and officially recognized in 2014 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. We were created with a simple vision: to ensure that no one should go without proper vision care due to cost or access limitations.

What started as a small group of passionate volunteers has grown into a thriving organization that serves mission partners across 20+ countries. Every single member of our team is a volunteer, dedicated to making a global impact.
